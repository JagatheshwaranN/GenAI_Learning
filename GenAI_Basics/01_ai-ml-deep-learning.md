# Artificial Intelligence (AI)

Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the field of computer science focused on building systems capable of performing tasks that typically require human intelligence.

These tasks include:

- Learning from data
- Reasoning and decision making
- Understanding natural language
- Recognizing images
- Solving complex problems
- Recognizing patterns

### Simple Definition

> Artificial Intelligence is the simulation of human intelligence in machines.

### Examples of AI Applications

Artificial Intelligence powers many modern systems such as:

- Voice assistants
- Recommendation systems
- Fraud detection
- Autonomous vehicles
- Medical diagnosis systems
- Chatbots

# Machine Learning (ML)

Machine Learning (ML) is a **subset of Artificial Intelligence**.

It enables computers to **learn from data and improve their performance over time without being explicitly programmed**.

Instead of writing rules manually, the system learns patterns from historical data.

### Definition

Machine Learning is a method of training computers to **learn from data and make predictions or decisions without being explicitly programmed**.

### Example Applications

- Email spam filtering
- Recommendation systems (Netflix, Amazon)
- Fraud detection
- Predictive analytics

### Example

A spam detection system can learn from thousands of labeled emails and automatically identify spam messages.

### Key Concept

```
Data + Algorithms → Learned Patterns
```

These learned patterns help models make predictions on **new unseen data**.

# Deep Learning

Deep Learning is a **subset of Machine Learning** that uses **Artificial Neural Networks with many layers** to solve complex problems.

These neural networks are inspired by the structure of the **human brain**.

Deep learning models are highly effective in processing **large amounts of unstructured data** such as text, images, and audio.

---

# Human Brain vs Neural Networks

| Human Brain | Deep Learning |
|-------------|---------------|
| Uses neurons | Uses artificial neurons |
| Neurons transmit signals | Neural networks process data |
| Learns from experience | Learns from large datasets |

---

# Applications of Deep Learning

Deep learning is particularly powerful in areas such as:

- Natural Language Processing
- Image recognition
- Speech recognition
- Autonomous systems

### Example

Large AI systems such as **ChatGPT** are built using deep learning models.
